About Us

Image

Founded in 2004 by Shalini Vidya Samstha, Jyothishmathi represents a rich tradition of excellence in technology-based education. A premier-league institution among the affiliates of JNTUH, our college owes its vision to Sri Sagar Rao, a veteran statesman of Independent India.

Our Vision:

We at Jyothishmathi, will impart futuristic technical education and instill high patterns of discipline through our dedicated staff that shall set global standards, making our students technologically superior and ethically strong, who in turn shall improve the quality of life of the human race.
