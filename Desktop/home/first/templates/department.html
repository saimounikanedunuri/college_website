The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was formed with the primary objective of providing world class education in the field of Information Technology. Right from its inception, the Department has been offering excellent infrastructural facilities with a variety of computing platforms to aspiring professional students to meet the burgeoning demands of the IT industry.
